About AquaWorld

Your Trusted Partner in Aquarium Hobby

Our Story

Founded in 2020, AquaWorld began with a simple mission: to make aquarium keeping accessible and enjoyable for everyone in India. What started as a small passion project has grown into a trusted online destination for aquarium enthusiasts nationwide.

Our Mission

We provide high-quality aquatic life, aquariums, and accessories while educating our customers on proper aquarium care. We believe in sustainable practices and ethical sourcing of all our aquatic creatures.

What We Offer

  • Live Fish (Guppy, Betta, Koi, Catfish)
  • Small Turtles & Aquatic Pets
  • Premium Aquariums & Tanks
  • Aquarium Stones, Sand & Decorations
  • Filters, Pumps & Maintenance Equipment
  • Fish Food & Health Products
